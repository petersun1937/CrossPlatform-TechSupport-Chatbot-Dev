Technical FAQ & Guidelines

Welcome to our product support page! Below, you’ll find answers to common questions and guidelines for using our product effectively.

1. What are the system requirements?
Operating System: Windows 10 or later, macOS 10.13 or later
Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent
Memory: 4 GB RAM or more
Storage: At least 500 MB free space
Internet: A stable internet connection for software updates and online features
2. How do I install the product?
For Windows:
Download the installer from [our website].
Open the downloaded file and follow the installation wizard.
Once installed, launch the application from your desktop.
For macOS:
Download the DMG file from [our website].
Open the file and drag the application to your "Applications" folder.
Open the application from "Applications" to get started.
3. How do I update the product?
Our product supports automatic updates. If there’s a new version available:

You’ll receive a prompt within the application to download and install the latest version.
Alternatively, you can check for updates manually by going to Settings > Check for Updates.
4. What should I do if the product crashes or freezes?
Ensure your system meets the minimum requirements listed above.
Restart the application and try again.
If the issue persists, restart your computer.
If you continue to experience issues, contact our support team at [email] with details of the problem.
5. How do I reset the product to default settings?
To reset to default settings:

Go to Settings > Reset to Default.
Confirm your choice, and the product will restore to factory settings. Note that this will erase all custom configurations.
6. How do I contact support?
For further assistance, reach out to our support team at [email] or call us at [phone number]. We are available Monday to Friday, from 9 AM to 5 PM.

7. Can I transfer my license to another device?
Yes, you can transfer your license to a different device by:

Deactivating the license on your current device (found in Settings > License).
Install the product on the new device and activate the license using your key.
